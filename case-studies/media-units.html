The Challenge

The objective was to maximize portfolio revenue by establishing a repeatable, strategic design validation process. The primary friction point was the "Tab-unit"—a core product that was underperforming its potential due to complex initial concepts and low visual guidance.

Design Validation

The Framework

I established the Design Validation Framework for the entire media portfolio, leading analytics-driven feature iteration to achieve measurable metric uplift.

Iterative Loops

By combining multivariate testing with usability prototypes, we bridged the gap between raw data and traveler intent.

V1 Iteration: Optimized via user research for functionality

Strategic Details

A single, high-leverage design change—a subtle bounce animation on a directional arrow—successfully created the visual guidance needed to more than double the initial click-through rate.

Hype Animation: Demonstrating micro-interaction impact on conversion

Portfolio Expansion

Scalability across Partners

Following the success of the Tab-unit, I designed a range of other products—from complex widgets to sleek Expedia-branded units and highly effective micro-units built on the same core principle of adding value.
