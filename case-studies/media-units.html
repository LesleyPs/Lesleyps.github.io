The Challenge

The objective was to maximize portfolio revenue by establishing a repeatable, strategic design validation process. The primary friction point was the "Tab-unit"—a core product that was underperforming its potential.

Design Validation

From Proof-of-Concept to Performance

Our initial version served as a successful technical proof-of-concept, but lacked the UX refinement needed to drive high-velocity engagement. By analyzing early click-through rates and user friction points, we evolved the design to prioritize clarity and conversion.

Strategic Details

A single, high-leverage design change—a subtle bounce animation on a directional arrow—successfully created the visual guidance needed to more than double the initial click-through rate.

Portfolio Expansion

Expanding the design system across diverse product verticals while maintaining a unified visual language and interaction model.
