Product Design Process

Working across a diverse ecosystem (Roku, Xbox, Apple TV, iOS), I focused on defining a global IA and navigation system that unified content discovery.

  • • Defined global IA for unified discovery.
  • • Specialized layouts for 10-foot interfaces.
  • • Created foundational component library concepts.

The 10-Foot Experience

Designing for the living room requires a different mental model. I designed user flows that respected the native experience of each platform.

A: High-Fidelity CTV Layout Strategy
B: Hype Animation / Interaction Demo

In-Person Validation

I led in-person usability sessions, observing users in real-time as they navigated our Hi-Fi prototypes to provide qualitative pivots that informed final production.

Strategic Conversion

Instead of a traditional feature list, I proposed a radical shift for the sign-up flow: a simple call to action to "unlock more for free."

The Outcome

Validated through usability and A/B testing, this approach focused on emphasizing immediate value and reducing cognitive friction.
