Engagement: Before & After

Before: Static Show Discovery
In the baseline version of the Show Page, content discovery was passive. While this area received the highest volume of traffic, there was no native mechanism to convert users into registered members without interrupting their viewing flow.

Baseline: High-traffic show page without registration touchpoint.

After: Contextual Implementation
I implemented a dynamic registration touchpoint directly into the Show Page. This animated trigger captures users during their primary flow, providing a frictionless path to registration that upholds the "Hassle-Free" brand promise.

Implementation: Contextual, animated touchpoint for universal interaction.

Ecosystem Cohesion

To maintain "Studio-Quality" authority across mobile, web, and OTT, I focused on unifying the focus-routing logic and typographic hierarchy. By matching user intent to the specific platform hardware—whether it's a tactile mobile swipe or a 10-foot D-pad interaction—I ensured the "Hassle-Free" brand identity remained consistent across every touchpoint in the Sony Crackle ecosystem.
