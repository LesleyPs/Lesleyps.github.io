The core challenge was to improve the streaming experience for a free, ad-supported service. We needed to encourage users to create an account for enhanced features like watchlists and personalized recommendations, overcoming the inherent friction of signing up while providing a valuable, consistent experience across platforms.

My Role & Solution
My process was grounded in collaborative and data-driven methodologies, always focusing on the user. As a UX/UI designer, I was a key contributor to the design team responsible for the core streaming experience. My work focused on improving key areas like Information Architecture and flow, while also helping to create a cohesive visual identity across all platforms. A key part of this process included brainstorming with the team, card sorting, and in-person user testing. A prime example was redesigning the sign-up flows.  I recommended an out-of-the-box approach: instead of listing features, I proposed a simple call to action to "unlock more for free." I validated this design through A/B testing, which proved this approach was more effective. I designed user flows that worked with the native experience of each platform, from Roku and Sony PlayStation to Xbox, Apple TV, and iOS.
