Control Tower
Solution
A tool that streamlined onboarding with intuitive, step-by-step flows for independent setup, enabling partners to configure media units, control their display frequency, and view key performance metrics. 
We needed to scale by enabling smaller partners to self-onboard and manage campaigns, which previously consumed significant manual and developer resources. Our goal was to build a new platform for partner activation and performance insights.
